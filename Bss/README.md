# Bee Swarm Simulator Clone

A 3D web game inspired by Roblox's Bee Swarm Simulator, built with Three.js.

## How to Play

1.  **Move**: Use `WASD` or `Arrow Keys`.
2.  **Collect Pollen**: Walk near flowers and click the mouse to swing your tool.
3.  **Convert Honey**: Walk to the Big Gold Hive at the back of the map to convert your Pollen into Honey.
4.  **Hatch Bees**: Use Honey to buy Eggs from the shop button. Bees will follow you and automatically gather pollen!

## Features

*   3D World with Player and Camera control.
*   Interactive Flowers and Hive.
*   Pollen gathering and Honey conversion loop.
*   Shop system to purchase Bees.
*   AI Bees that follow and gather resources.
